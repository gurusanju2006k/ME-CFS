# NeuroWell AI: ME/CFS and Depression Prediction System

## Project Overview

NeuroWell AI is a Machine Learning-based healthcare application designed to predict the likelihood of Myalgic Encephalomyelitis/Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (ME/CFS) and Depression using user-provided symptoms and health-related information.

The system helps users gain preliminary insights into their mental and physical health conditions through an easy-to-use web interface. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, the platform provides quick and efficient predictions that can support awareness and early screening.

---

## Problem Statement

ME/CFS and Depression are often difficult to identify in their early stages because many symptoms overlap with other health conditions. Delayed recognition can affect treatment and quality of life.

This project aims to provide a machine learning-based solution that analyzes symptoms and predicts the possibility of ME/CFS and Depression, helping users become more aware of their health status.
